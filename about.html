Cross-Country Analysis of Microfinance

This Research paper involves a detailed analysis of microfinance institutions across various countries. The objective is to uncover trends, challenges, and opportunities within the microfinance sector, offering valuable insights for policymakers, investors, and practitioners. By utilizing R for data visualization and statistical modeling, I aim to identify patterns and correlations in microfinance data.

Key Features:

- Data collection and preprocessing from multiple sources. -Statistical analysis to identify key trends. -Visualizations to present findings effectively.
