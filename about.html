Tech Strategy Visualizations

This project involves developing an interactive dashboard using Shiny to visualize technology strategy data. The goal is to create a user-friendly platform that allows stakeholders to explore technology adoption trends, identify areas for improvement, and make informed strategic decisions. The dashboard integrates data from various sources, enabling users to customize their analysis and gain actionable insights.

Key Features:

  • Interactive visualizations for real-time data exploration.
  • User inputs to filter and customize data views.
  • Comprehensive analysis of technology adoption trends.

Cross-Country Analysis of Microfinance

This research paper involves a detailed analysis of microfinance institutions across various countries. The objective is to uncover trends, challenges, and opportunities within the microfinance sector, offering valuable insights for policymakers, investors, and practitioners. By utilizing R for data visualization and statistical modeling, the aim is to identify patterns and correlations in microfinance data.

Key Features:

  • Data visualization techniques to highlight relationships between microfinance variables.
  • Statistical modeling to identify factors influencing microfinance success.
  • Trend identification to analyze historical shifts in microfinance practices.
  • Policy recommendations based on data analysis.
  • Stakeholder engagement through interactive data exploration.
  • Comprehensive reports summarizing key findings and recommendations.
